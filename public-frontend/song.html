Transcript

▼

"Freddie came to us with this six-minute epic that defied every convention in rock music. He had the whole thing mapped out in his head — the ballad section, the operatic part, the hard rock finale. We all thought he was mad, honestly.

We recorded it at Rockfield Studios in Wales. The vocal overdubs alone took three weeks. We'd stack harmony upon harmony until the tape was literally wearing thin. Roger's voice would go up to notes none of us could reach.

The record label said it was too long, too weird, would never be a single. Kenny Everett played it on his radio show fourteen times in one weekend. The rest is history."

What We're Building

Storyteller is a story distribution platform for the music industry. Artists record short audio narratives about their songs, and Storyteller distributes those stories to streaming platforms as a new content type.

Think of it like DistroKid, but for the stories behind songs instead of the songs themselves.

How It Works

1
Record
Artist records or uploads a story about their song
›
2
Package
Storyteller links it to the track and packages it with metadata
›
3
Distribute
Story assets are pushed to streaming platforms via API
›
4
Listen
Listener plays a song — the story plays first, then the track begins

Why This Matters

Streaming killed liner notes. Every song has a story — its inspiration, meaning, how it was made — but that context disappeared when music went digital. Spotify's "About the Song" feature shows text cards. Storyteller brings the stories back as audio, integrated into the listening experience.

Who Uses It

Listeners never touch Storyteller. They experience stories through their existing streaming app.

What We're Building (v1)

Artist & Label Portal

Web app + Mobile app

  • Artist sign-up and identity verification
  • Browse catalog (tracks linked by ISRC)
  • Bulk catalog import (CSV upload)
  • Record a story directly in the app
  • Upload pre-recorded story audio
  • Link stories to specific tracks
  • Verify, edit, replace, or delete stories
  • Story status dashboard
  • Notifications (distributed, flagged, etc.)

Backend Platform

Core services + Distribution API

  • Story management (create, edit, version)
  • Track-to-story mapping via ISRC
  • Artist verification workflow
  • Story prioritization engine
  • Content moderation & review queue
  • Distribution API for streaming partners
  • Webhook notifications on changes
  • Admin / ops dashboard
  • Full audit trail for compliance

Designed For Future (Not Built in v1)

The architecture accommodates these — they plug in without rebuilding anything:

  • AI narrative generation
  • AI voice likeness narration
  • AI safety guardrails
  • Archival compilation from interviews
  • Lyrics validation engine
  • Instrumental crossfade
  • Analytics dashboard
  • Interactive storytelling

All future features are covered in the patent filing and expand the platform's value over time.

Revenue Model

Storyteller generates revenue as the distribution layer for story content across the streaming industry.

ModelDescription
Platform LicensingStreaming platforms pay to access the story catalog (annual license per platform)
Per-Stream RoyaltySmall fee per story play ($0.001 – $0.005 per stream)
Artist / Label SubscriptionMonthly fee for premium features (analytics, priority support, bulk tools)
Content LicensingLabels pay for AI story generation across their catalog (future)

Market context: Global music streaming is ~$40B/year with ~5 trillion annual streams. Even a fraction of a cent per story play at scale is significant. Platform licensing to 4 major services alone could be substantial.

Artist Onboarding

Artists must be verified before stories can be distributed. Three paths:

MethodHow It Works
Label InvitationLabel manager invites artist by email. Label vouches for identity.
Distributor LinkingArtist connects existing DistroKid/TuneCore account to verify catalog.
Manual ReviewIndependent artists without a label submit for admin verification.

Artists can also bulk import their catalog via CSV upload (ISRC + title). Future: direct distributor API integration.

First Steps

A clear path from idea to working product.

  1. File the provisional patent — Establishes priority date and IP protection. Cost: $75–150 at USPTO.
  2. Form legal entity — LLC or equivalent. Ownership/equity agreement between founders.
  3. Finalize tech stack — Confirm backend language/framework, mobile framework, and cloud provider.
  4. Hire developers — 2–3 developers: backend + frontend/mobile. Freelance or agency.
  5. Build v1 — Artist portal (web + mobile), story management backend, distribution API. ~3–4 months.
  6. Onboard first artists — Use the label partner's roster to populate the platform with real stories.
  7. Approach first streaming platform — Demo real stories from real artists on a working platform.
